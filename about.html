About

We're a sing-along band in Tucson, AZ singing fun-loving uplifting songs and chants. Find us at*:

  • Riverfront Park Thursday mornings at Chi Dance Chant (see schedule). First half hour is ecstatic dance, followed by an hour or so of singing and chanting until we get hungry.
  • Northwest Global Chant at Oro Valley United Church of Christ, 1st, 3rd, and 5th Mondays at 6:30pm.
  • Sunset Sundays at James Kreigh Park, at quarter-till-sunset we dance with the sunset and end with meditative music while lying on yoga mats watching the stars emerge. We call it a mini camping ecstatic dance trip.
  • Usually jamming with other musicians and friends after Spirit of Movement Tucson's Ecstatic-type Dance at Himmel Park, Sunday mornings (see their site for details).
  • We also have a WhatsApp group; the community is "Oro Valley dance and chant", and the group is "Musical celebrations singing and dancing in Oro Valley mostly".

* Check back for time changes with the seasons or postponed dates.
